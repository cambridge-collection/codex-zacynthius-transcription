45 For why has so great a good been performed for me “that the mother of my Lord comes to me?” *
She calls the one who is still a virgin a mother, anticipating the fulfilment by a prophetic word. So
on one hand the divine dispensation brought witness to Elizabeth, so that the witness
of John to the Lord even from the womb should be fulfilled. Yet the visit of the Virgin to her relation
had its own eagerness. For she came to see Elizabeth and the miraculous
embryo in her, according to the speech of the angel, so that through this, childbirth
was given greater credence. And, I say, what came from the Virgin herself and what is consistent with
this faith is the word of Elizabeth, saying “And blessed is the she who believed that there would be
a fulfilment of what was spoken to her from the Lord.” *❧
