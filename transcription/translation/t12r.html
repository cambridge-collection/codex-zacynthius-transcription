From Isidore the Elder of Pelusium, Letter 363:

55 Let not this prophecy of Elizabeth disturb you from which she says this to me, “ that the
Mother of the Lord should come to me. ” For the one who established in her the forming of the baptist
even before he came forth from his mother to be a prophet made the forerunner holy, for
he provided the revelation of the mystery inside him, since he did not grant embryos the power of
speech at an unseasonable time. His mother used the instrument of her mouth for the baptist
hurrying to proclaim the redeemer. ❧
