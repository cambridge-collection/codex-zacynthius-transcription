64 After Zechariah wrote on the writing tablet that“ his name will be John” ,
the restriction of his voice was loosened and he received back his speech. It was no longer
restricted as before in his unbelief, but it blessed God and prophesied
what is written here: first with regard to Christ, how he was already present in this
life, when he said: “ Blessed is the Lord, the God of Israel, for he has examined and made a
redemption for his people” , and then with regard to John. ❧
