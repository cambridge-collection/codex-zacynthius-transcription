64 After Zechariah wrote on the writing tablet that “His name will be John,” * his voice
which had been bound was loosened and he received back his speech. It was no longer
bound as before in his unbelief, but it blessed God and prophesied what is written
here: first with regard to Christ, as if he had already been present in this life, when
he said: “Blessed be the Lord, the God of Israel, for he has looked favourably on
his people and redeemed them” *, and then with regard to John.❧
