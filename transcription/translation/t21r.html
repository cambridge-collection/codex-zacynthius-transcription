86 I think that the Lord is for falling and rising, not when some fall and others
rise, but when the same person stands better apart. For while the appearance
of the Lord is destructive of bodily sufferings, it stimulates
the specific features of the soul. As it is when Paul says: “ When I am weak, then
I am powerful. ” The same person is weak and powerful, but while he is weak in the flesh, he is
powerful in the Spirit. Thus too the Lord does not provide some people the instigation for falling,
and others for rising. For those who fall from the state in which they once were
