2:36 +144There was also a prophet, Anna
the daughter of Phanuel of the tribe
of Asher. She was of great age,
having lived with her husband
for seven years after her
marriage, 2:37then as a
widow to the age of eighty
four. She never left the temple
but worshipped there with
fasting and prayer day
and night. 2:38At that moment
she came, and began to praise
God and to speak about the child to
all who were looking for the redemption
of Jerusalem.  2:39 +15When they had
finished everything required by the
law of the Lord they returned to
Galilee to the town of their own of
Nazaret.
