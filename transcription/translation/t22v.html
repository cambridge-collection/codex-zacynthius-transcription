88 It hints that after the scandal at the cross of Christ which comes
both on the disciples and Mary herself, a certain healing will follow on swiftly
from the Lord, which makes the heart firm for belief in him. For
thus we see that Peter
too clung fast to faith
in Christ. And so the
unsound human element
is disgraced so that the strength
of the Lord may be shown. ❧
