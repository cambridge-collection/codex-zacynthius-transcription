13 So admirable and great was John that the crowds of the Jews
came to suggest that, somehow, he himself might be the Christ, who had been written through the
shadow of the law and had been heralded in advance through the holy prophets.
But when once some people advanced into this type of suggestion, he cuts off the
suspicion like a servant stepping aside from the honours of his master. For the distance
between God and Human is immeasurable. ❧        From the same ❧
