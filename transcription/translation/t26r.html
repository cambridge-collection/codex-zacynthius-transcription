22 Now, observe for me his Human nature, how by the grace of the Holy Spirit it was sufficient
among the first fruits for the Christ and it was garlanded with honours from the highest.
For long ago, the God of all made a promise, saying that: “ It shall come to pass in those
days that I pour out from my Spirit on all flesh. ” This is fulfilled for us,
as what was promised happened in first place to Christ. And concerning the older ones
who had declined unchecked to a love of the flesh, Christ says somewhere: “ My Spirit does not
› rest on these Humans because they are flesh. ” But when everything
› has become new in Christ and we have been enriched by rebirth through the Spirit and
water, we will no longer have dealings with children of flesh and blood. Rather,
we will call God our Father, and we will very suitably be those who are honoured
