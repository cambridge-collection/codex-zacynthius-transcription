22 Now I ask you to look at how human nature is anointed by the grace of the
Holy Spirit in Christ as first fruits and crowned with the highest honours.

pass in those days that I pour out out my Spirit on all flesh.” * What was promised

of his assumption of our likeness, because he transferred to himself our poverty, he
is sanctified with us even though he himself sanctifies the whole creation, so that
he should not appear to be refusing the measure of humanity. He did not flee from
being born human for the sake of the salvation and the life of all, and was born
in our likeness in every respect whatever apart from sin alone.❧
