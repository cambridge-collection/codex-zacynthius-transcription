23 What, then, is“ He was driven” ? Not rather that he was carried away, but that he spent time and made his
dwelling. For somehow we ourselves also have the habit of talking about each living thing in
a likeness: “ Such a one perchance drives himself well. ” So he made his dwelling
Lin the desert in the Spirit: this means spiritually. For he had fasted,
giving absolutely nothing as nourishment for the need of the body. He was not himself
in need of fasting, but being a model for us he set up his own affairs as an decree
and was made an image of an exceptional and marvellous life
among us. For from where could we know the people on the earth, that loving to spend time in deserted places is beneficial for them and necessary for salvation?
For they go away, as if it were from the waves and storms and the distractions that there are
in this present life. And, according to the Blessed Joseph, in a single night
