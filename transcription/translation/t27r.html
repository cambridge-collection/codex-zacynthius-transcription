26 The devil introduces
a third temptation,
of vanity.
But he is cast out.
And take notice of
this: “ For it is written, ”
he says, “ You shall not put
the Lord your God to the test. ”
For God does not
grant aid to those
who tempt, but
to those who believe
in him. For he does
not consider us worthy
of being spared. Through
this we owe an exhibit
to these people that
Christ has never
given himself as
a sign for those who
tempt. For“ a wicked
and adulterous generation
asks for a sign,
and a sign shall not be given to it. ” May the tempter, Satan, hear this now
too. Accordingly, we have been victorious in Christ, and the one who once ruled in Adam
has departed in shame, so that we should have him under our feet. For when Christ
was victorious, then he sent to us as well the ability to be victorious. “ See, ”
he says, “ I have given you power to trample on snakes and scorpions and on all the power
of the enemy. ” Observe how he mischievously wishes through deploying the Scriptures
