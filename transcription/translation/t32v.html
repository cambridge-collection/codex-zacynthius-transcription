14 On Levi the Tax Collector* From Saint Cyril.❧

54 Levi was a tax collector, a man insatiable for financial gain, unbridled in acquisitiveness,
an unjust desirer of what did not belong to him: for this is the habit of tax collectors.
But he was snatched away from among the workers of injustice and amazingly he was
saved when Christ, the Saviour of us all, called him. For he said to him, “Follow me,” *
and the other man abandoned everything and followed him. You see how truly the
most wise Paul says that “Christ came into the world to save sinners.” *? You see
how the incarnate first-born Word of God transferred the possessions of the devil
to himself? And while
