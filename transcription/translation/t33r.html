55 For the Saviour of all, since he is indeed the physician of spirits, does not desert
those who need him. As he is able to cleanse, he purposefully took up residence
with those who had not yet been purified. But, Pharisee, let us see the pride of your
named Levi sitting at the tax
booth; and he said to him,
‘Follow me.’ 5:28And he got up,
left everything,   and followed
