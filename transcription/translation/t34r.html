57 Observe for me again how Christ shows them up. They have not taken part in the festival
but they have no share in the delight at it, but they lie outside the global
feast. For it is a feast and nothing else, the revealing of our Saviour
to this world, just as it deliberately joins Human nature to him
like a spouse, so that the woman who has long been barren should become fruitful
and most productive. So they are friends of the bridegroom, all those who have been
called by him through the new proclamantion of the good news. But the scribes
