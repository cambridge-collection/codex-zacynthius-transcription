74 Having revealed that poverty through God of every good thing, and hunger and weeping
will not be without reward for the saints, saying: “ He changes the word to what is
the opposite for them and he shows that these belong to punishment and produce judgment, ”
so that they may be captured by the desire for garlands on their labours,
and, choosing poverty through God through the fear of the impending
punishment, they may flee wealth and living in luxury and laughter, meaning
in worldly joys. And he says that some will be inheritors of the kingdom
of the heavens, while others will fail into final misfortunes. For,
