From Saint Cyril
79￣ He cuts off every difficult suffering from our thoughts, the beginning and origin
of contempt. For indeed it is necessary for certain people to examine themselves and order their lives
according to God, they do not do this but they meddle with other people’s business.
If they see anyone unwell, they approach them as a way of forgetting their own
weaknesses. They make money the pretext for censoriousness, and the starting-point
for slander. For they condemn them, not knowing that they are just as
sick as those who suffer at their hands. They pass sentence on themselves.
The most wise Paul also writes somewhere in this way: “ For in the manner that you judge someone
is merciful.
