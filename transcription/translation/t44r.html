90￣ This is also the source of our comfort about those who die,
as we hold the resurrection before our eyes. But these people rose again on the point of death. “ Christ
once risen dies no more: death no longer has dominion over him. ” For he has become
for us the first fruits
of the resurrection
and the first-born
from the dead, and
life according to nature.
But the miracle is why
he did not accomplish this
with only a word, but he
also touched the bier, so
that you might learn how
effective the holy body of
Christ is for the salvation
of a Human. For the body
is of life and the flesh
is of the all-powerful
word, and it clothed
his power. For just
as when iron meets
fire, the power of
the fire makes it
effective and
finishes its surface,
so it is when the flesh
of the one who
instilled life in all
things became that of
the word: through this
it is life-giving itself and destroys death and destruction. ❧
