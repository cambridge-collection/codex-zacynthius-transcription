99￣ John had such a reputation, and was awesome in his origins, noble
in his appearance, holy in his purpose, unimpeachable in his life, pure
in his intention, spirit-bearing in his grace, powerful in all things and
noble in every way. For he was the son of the chief priest, and awesome to the people,
as he was compared to those who had previously departed this life for God. While he was great,
nevertheless he still faced reversals in life: he had not yet received the promised hope.
