From Saint Cyril
3 He had been invited by one of the Pharisees, but since he is kind and
benevolent and wishes all Humans to be saved and to come to the knowledge of the truth,
he assented to the one who asked a favour and entered and took his seat. Then a woman
came up. She had led a licentious life, but she displayed a trustworthy
choice. And, not realising the disgrace among those who had already arrived, she called to
Christ, as he was able to wipe away and banish every accusation, and grant release
from all misdemeanours. ❧
