* From Saint Titus
26￣ “The word of God” *: the word was his, for there is one teaching from the Father and the Son in
the prophets and in the evangelists through the activity of the Holy Spirit. But he says “the word of
God” in order to direct the honour to the Father. For as it befits the Father to beget
a Son, so it befits the one who is begotten to honour the begetter. However, his mother
and brothers “are those who hear the word of God and do it.” * For hearing
also needs to be put into practice.❧
