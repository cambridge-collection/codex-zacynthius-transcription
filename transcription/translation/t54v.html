From Saint Titus
26￣ The word of God was his word. For there is one teaching from the Father and the Son in the prophets
and in the evangelists through the activity of the holy Spirit. But he says the word of
God in order to send back the honour to the Father. For as it befits the Father to beget
a Son, so it befits the one who is begotten to honour the begetter. However, his Mother
and brothers are these who hear and carry out the word of God. For hearing
also requires practice. ❧
