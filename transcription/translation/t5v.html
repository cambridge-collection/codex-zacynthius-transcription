From Origen


13 Perhaps someone will ask why the word“ blameless” is attached to them. For it would have been sufficient for them to have travelled
in all the ways of God and in righteousness. Unless it is possible to travel in all
the commandments, but not blamelessly. How, then, someone says, is it possible to travel in all
the commandments, but not blamelessly? Well, I say to them that unless this was the case, it
would not have said, “ Pursue what is right righteously” whenever we might do the commandments, so that
there should not be in our conscience the filth of vanity or human obsequiousness or of another thing
of this nature as we do the commandments blamelessly. ❧
