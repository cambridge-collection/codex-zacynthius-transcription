From Saint Cyril
51￣ And I have examined carefully what“ Send them away” means. For, as I said, some of those who followed
him were urging him to free them from the evil spirits which possessed them,
while others were
seeking the banishing
of their sicknesses.
The disciples, then,
knowing that simply
by nodding he would
accomplish what was
desired by the afflicted
say“ Send them away” .
This is not because
they themselves
were weary and
thought that the
opportunity was past,
but they had love for
the crowds and were
already fulfilling their
pastoral role and
beginning to care
for the people. ❧
