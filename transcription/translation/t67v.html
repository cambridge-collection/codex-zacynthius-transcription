From Saint Titus

60￣ On the mountain, the old covenant and the new were joined together. The apostles made common cause with
the prophets and together they beheld the glory of the king: “ This is
my Son” , about whom Moses prophesied, as did the prophets; “ This is the chosen
one” , chosen not from many, but when he even became Human: “ Listen
to him. ” Let Moses be silent in future, let the prophets be silent. Listen to him, the one who
was prophesied and has arrived, that it is necessary to suffered, that it is necessary to be
crucified, that it is necessary to be scorned and to share the death of Humans, that it is necessary to rise
on behalf of Humans, that it is necessary to fulfil the entire dispensation, that it is necessary that
you yourselves are also tested and suffer much so that you may be sharers in glory. For it is this
glory which you have seen but are unable to share unless you suffer with the one who has been
glorified. ❧             From Saint Cyril
