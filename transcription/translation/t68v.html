62￣ But the voice of God the Father is fine and astounding. Already possessed by
sheer terror, the disciples fall down so that again we might also learn through this
that this is most necessary for those on earth. The spiritual mediation of our Saviour
has been shown forth, clearly by the manner of his incarnation. For if he had not
become like us, who could have borne God speaking to us from above and his
indescribable glory when he appears, which perhaps no creature can endure?
For, as the divine Paul said, “He dwells in unapproachable light.” *❧ *
