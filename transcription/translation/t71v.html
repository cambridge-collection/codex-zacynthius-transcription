70￣ Did the pang of envy strike the holy disciples? Do they begrudge those who are
favoured? Tell me, do you forbid in the name of Christ the one who crushes evil spirits?
Yet how should you not rather have considered that it was not that person themselves who was the
doer of the marvel, but the grace within them which performed the marvel, clearly in the
power of Christ. How is it, then? Do you forbid the one who excels in Christ? ‘Yes’, he says,
for “he does not follow with us.” * What does it matter if the one crowned by grace from Christ has
not become numbered among the holy apostles? Very many are the diversities of the
› gifts from Christ. The blessed Paul will also teach this, saying that “To one is given
