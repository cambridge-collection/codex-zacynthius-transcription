From Saint Cyril

93 Let us examine above everything else what is meant by“ He rejoiced in the Holy Spirit. ” For
while the holy spirit proceeds as if from a spring of the God and Father, it is not alien to
the Son. For all the individuality of the Father belongs to the word which was born from him by nature
and in truth. Accordingly, he saw through the effectiveness of the Spirit which he had given to
those who were worthy, whom he also ordered to minister the divine proclamation. He saw that many had
been drawn into the net; signs worthy of wonder had been accomplished through them, and finally
that what was under the heaven was in the beginnings of salvation through him, I mean salvation through
faith. And for this reason he rejoiced in the holy Spirit, meaning at the
effectiveness and power which came through the holy Spirit, knowing well that those who were sent by him
