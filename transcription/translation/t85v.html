3￣ The manner of hospitality is a great matter and worthy of acquisition. The most wise
Paul also bears witness to this, writing “Do not forget to be hospitable.” * Martha
was such a person
when she entertained
the Lord: it is a fine
thing to imitate her,
just like her sister Mary
as well, who was most
fond of learning and
who sat at the feet of
the Saviour and filled
her mind with the
teachings from him.❧
