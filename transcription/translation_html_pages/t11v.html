So, they say, how could the body of Emmanuel not be from Mary? Fool! But
you have rejected the womb which produced the blessed and true fruit,
and you invent an apparition, the mother of some non-existent phantom, and you make
our salvation a phantom and a dream. For if woman was of the same substance as Adam, or
was taken from his flank and created, and sin is derived from her,
erring in the transgression of the commandment with him, the Virgin too
was in all ways of the the same substance. From her, Christ, the second Adam, has risen up, who takes away the
sin of the world, so that“ those who have died in Adam might be made alive in Christ”

45 For why has so great a good been done for me that the Mother of my Lord should come to me?
She calls the one who is still a virgin a Mother, anticipating the delivery in a prophetic word. So
while the divine ordering brought forth testimony to Elizabeth, so that the testimony
of John from his mother to the Lord should also be fulfilled through his own
mother, the visit of the Virgin to her relative
had its haste. For she came to see Elizabeth and the paradoxical
embryo in her, according to the voice of the angel, so that through this the childbirth
might be believed more greatly. I say that what came from the virgin herself and ran to meet this
faith was the word of Elizabeth saying“ And blessed is the woman who believes that there shall be
a complete of the things that are spoken to her from the Lord. ❧
