[...] From the holy Spirit and this one was that ancient Jesus (Joshua) again as a model, who
went into battle with Moses. For he earlier made the sons of Israel cross over
the Jordan, and then straightaway he sat and circumcised them with stone knives. For is it not so when we
cross over the Jordan, then Christ circumcises us in the power of the Spirit: he does not
cleanse the flesh but rather cuts out the defilement in souls. Accordingly, in us
Christ circumcises and takes on the closing, as I have said. For then,
then we have been saved through him. ❧
From Severus Archbishop of Antioch, from the Letter to Princess Patricia. ❧
