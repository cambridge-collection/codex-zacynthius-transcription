1 One might call it good news, since not only fixed and immovable things are good and beyond
our value, but because it is also given to us with complete ease. For it was not by struggling and
sweating but only by being loved by God that we have received what we have received. ❧
1 Some define the gospel in this way: a gospel is an account comprising the report of
matters, according to the gospel because it benefits and gladdens the hearer from the point when
the message is received. ❧
Alternatively
1 An account comprising the presence of good. ❧        Alternatively
1 An account reporting that the expected good is present. ❧
2 The blessed Luke was a doctor in his craft, but he became also a doctor of souls, having risen from
the lesser to the greater. For he has left behind for us the written memorial of both the words
and the miracles of Jesus. God had granted this* instructed genuinely to Christ.
But alone of the other evangelists, he accurately made the writing of the gospel and
many of the matters on which the others ere silent. He it was who both remembered and recounted accurately,
on account of which the preface of this gospel writing proclaims the accuracy. ❧

1:1+113Since many have undertaken
to set down an orderly account
In in times past
many people proclaimed
prophecy, but
some of these
were false prophets
and others were truly prophets:
the gift was given to the people
of the differentiation of spirits, the gift from which the true prophet was judged as well as the false
one. In the same way now, too, in the new covenant, many wished to write gospels
but the trustworthy stewards did not approve everyone. Instead they chose some of them, and
before long the failed attempts of those who came to the composition of gospels without the gift
have fallen into oblivion. For Matthew did not make a failed attempt but wrote
from the Holy Spirit, just as Mark and John did also, and equally Luke as well. However,
those who wrote the Gospel according to the Egyptians and the Gospel
according to the Twelve failed in their attempts. Basilides also dared to write
the Gospel according to Basilides. On one hand, then, many made failed attempts( for it is said that there is also a Gospel
according to Thomas and one according to Matthias and many others) , but the church of God
prefers the four Gospels. ❧
