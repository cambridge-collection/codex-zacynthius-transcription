From Saint Cyril
1￣ Perhaps it was some sort of a game among the children of the Jews, which was like this.
A crowd of children was divided into two parts. There was confusion about what is joyful in life
and what is not
expected in its
affairs and the
terrible and sudden
change of some
things to others.
While some pipe,
others lament.
But those who lament
do not harmonise with
those who pipe and rejoice
nor with those who weep.
Those who have the pipe
make a pretence, then
they call to each other
something about what
they consider a matter
which they do not share.
Christ was important
enough to be something
of this nature experienced
by the peoples of the Jews
with those standing there.
