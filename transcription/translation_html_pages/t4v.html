Some take as probable that he wrote the gospel for a certain Theophilus. If we are all such
people as to be loved and befriended by God, we are Theophiloi (loved by God) . And no-one
loved by God is weak. For just as it is written about the people coming out of Egypt that
there was no weak person in their tribes, so I would say that everyone loved by God is
strong, possessing the strength and power which comes from God and his word.
And in this way a person will recognise the security which has been derived concerning the words, as they understand the
word of the gospels. The firmness of knowledge is now proclaimed, so that whatever that person
received through hearing, when they
encounter the same in writing
they will keep faith
more securely. ❧
