[. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .] The phrase“ you have found favour” was common. For other women also found favour before her. However, to say“ You will conceive”
was not yet common, but alone as a message to the virgin. The matter was alien. For“ You have found
favour with God. For this reason do not fear. ” For the indwelling of the Saviour is the removal of all fear. ❧
